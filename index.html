Our Mission


Gemhack is a two day virtual hack-a-thon aimed at addressing the challenges facing the gem and jewelry industry. Over two days, participants will come together, form teams, and put together the beginnings of new, disruptive solutions aimed at resolving the unique issues found in the industry.

Gemhack is an inclusive tech event that welcomes participants and mentors from both within and outside of the industry, as we believe a diverse audience produces the best solutions. Participants will leave Gemhack with the knowledge, insight, and connections to take their projects outside of Gemhack to drive real, disruptive change.
